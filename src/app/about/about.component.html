Welcome to Indivisual!

Here's how it works (it's simple!). Think instagram, but without "influencers", ads, and all the other bs. The goal of indivisual is to provide a social space online to share what makes you feel positive, and most like an individual - and to see what makes others feel the same about themselves.

Now that you're signed up, you can not only view others' posts, but you can create your own. Click on the "create post" button in the toolbar to get started!
